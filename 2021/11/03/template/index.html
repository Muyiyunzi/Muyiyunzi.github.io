序

启发于中式教育的思维，我们写英语作文时会有很多的模板可以套，那么写顶会论文能否也“套模板”呢~

所以笔者尝试在阅读论文的过程中收集一些常用的语料素材，用于论文写作。内容可能比较零散，但应该不妨碍使用。

按论文结构展开

注：语料内容均摘自CV顶会论文。

Abstract

  • The main challenge in (task name, e.g. monocular 3D detection) lies in…

  • by adopting/augmenting (module name), our method successfully alleviates…

  • （不太常用）In particular, in this work we show that, thanks to (module name), a (characteristic) architecture suffices to set new state-of-the-art results on the popular KITTI 3D benchmark.

1. Introduction

  • （不太常用）With the advent of (application), significant attention has been devoted in the (research area) to the (task name).

e.g. With the advent of autonomous driving, significant attention has been devoted in the (research area) to the (task name).

  • In recent years, with the development of (technologies in) (background area), numerous impressive methods are proposed for (accurate) (task name).

e.g. In recent years, with the development of technologies in computer vision and deep learning, numerous impressive methods are proposed for accurate 2D object detection.

  • We propose a novel (task name) framework, (framework name), which…

  • To address this issue, a large number of works leverage…

  • In the sequel, we take a closer look at the issue and show that…

  • Contributions:

    • Our contributions can be summarized into four-fold. (1)… (2)…
    • Our main contributions are three-fold. (1)…(2)…
    • To summarize, our main contributions are as follows.
    • This new detection framework enjoys the following advantages.

2. Related Work

  • To better…, prior arts typically…

3. Approach/(framework name)

  • First, we give an overview of our architecture (as well as…). We then detail our… which is the key component for…. Finally, we outline the details of…

3.*. Formulation

Inputs, Ground Truth, Outputs

4. Experiments

4.1. Implementation details

datasets, training, testing procedure, etc.

4.3. Discussion

  • This suggests that it is indeed effective to…(your module function)

5.Conclusion

Rewrite to abstract with some specific index, along with few analysis and discussion.

6. Future work, further discussion, acknowledgements, etc.

  • Thank (person name) for his careful proofreading.

英语使用方面的笔记

inter-：表示“在…之间，相互”。e.g. internet, interchangeable（可互换的）

intra-：表示“在内，内部”。e.g. intraparty（党内的）, intra-class（类内的）

resort to: 使用，诉诸。e.g. resort to two-stage method

intractable：难对付的

discrepancy：差异。e.g. discrepancy between the ground-truth and the prediction

-agnostic：不详的、未定义的，不关注某方面。e.g. data-agnostic，无论什么形式的data都能feed in这个model；input-agnostic，无论什么形式的输入都可以。

Analogously：类似地。e.g. Analogously to [11, 17], …

homogeneous：齐次的。另外和homogenous都有表示相同、相似的意思，但不加e的更偏重于生物学当中，一般也使用homogeneous
